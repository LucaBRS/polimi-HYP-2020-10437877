Association

Petshood is an association that deals with the administration and management of three kennels on the territory of Emilia Romagna. Born from the love of the founders for animals, its area of intervention is to combine the pursuit of animal welfare and responsible management of their spaces. We want to spread a culture of proper animal care and we propose a model of kennels as a place open to the public, a place of comparison with other animals.

History Values Financial report

History

History The association was founded in 2002 by a group of animal-lovers members.
Over the years it has become of such importance in the territory that it was entrusted with the management of the small kennel of Reggio Emilia. The association was created and was able to obtain the administration of the kennels of Parma and Modena (2008-2011). Petshood is currently managing a total of 50 animals including dogs and cats and about twenty volunteers.

Values

Petshood aims in the first place to fight stray dogs in the territory, that is caused mainly by the abandonment of pets and their mistreatment at home, and which often leads them to run away and to show aggressive behaviour towards their fellow human beings. This is why we also aim to facilitate adoptions by making each family aware of their pets problems and making pets’ health accessible to everybody.

Financial report

Petshood uses the funds collected for 82% for its social purpose, the animals, even increasing the percentage of what is indicated in the "Best Practice" of good associations (80% for its own social mission, 20% for the management and promotion of the association), this without the cost of employees and minimizing the costs of management and promotion of the Organization. The financial reports are controlled by accountants non-registered to Petshood.
